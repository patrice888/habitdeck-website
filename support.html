Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?
Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We never have access to it. You can export your data anytime from Settings.
How do I restore my data on a new phone?
Go to Settings → Export data on your old phone. Save the JSON file. On your new phone, go to Settings → Import data and select the file.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the App Store → tap your profile → Subscriptions → HabitDeck → Cancel. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.
Can I use HabitDeck on multiple devices?
Currently HabitDeck stores data locally per device. You can use the export/import feature to move data between devices.
How do I add a widget?
Long press on your home screen → tap the + button → search for HabitDeck → choose small or medium widget → add it. Then long press the widget to choose which habit or goal to display.
