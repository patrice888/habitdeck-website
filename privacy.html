Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

🔒 TL;DR — HabitDeck does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

HabitDeck ("we", "our", or "the app") is built with privacy at its core. We believe your personal data belongs to you, and only you. This privacy policy explains what data we handle and how.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers
  • Your habits, goals, or any content you create in the app

Data Stored Locally

All data you create in HabitDeck — your habits, goals, completions, streaks, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it using the built-in export feature.

In-App Purchases

HabitDeck Pro is available as an in-app purchase. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. RevenueCat is used to manage subscription status — it receives only an anonymous user identifier to verify your purchase status.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service we use is RevenueCat for purchase management. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data Export & Import

You can export all your data at any time from Settings → Export data. This creates a JSON file stored on your device that you control entirely. You can also import this data to restore your information.

Children's Privacy

HabitDeck does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal data at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@habitdeck.link
